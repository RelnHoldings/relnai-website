How We Think
Three principles.
Every product.

We don't ship features — we ship experiences. Every decision in our product process comes back to one of three commitments we've made to the people who use what we build.

01
Real Life, Amplified

We don't build escapism. We build tools that make the experiences you're already having richer, more visible, and more worth remembering. The game is already happening — we just bring the scoreboard.

02
Family Is the Product

Every feature we design imagines a parent cheering louder, a kid feeling seen, or a coach making better decisions with better data. The people using our apps are not users — they're families.

03
Earn the Reward

Achievements only mean something if they're earned. We design progression systems that make milestones feel genuinely deserved — by athletes, parents, and coaches who put in the time.

Origin — Family
A Name from Our Children

"Reln" is constructed from the names of our founder's children. Every app we build carries that weight — a reminder that this work exists because families like ours deserve better tools.

Origin — Science
The Reelin Gene

Reelin is the protein encoded by the RELN gene — responsible for brain cell migration, synaptic plasticity, and how we form lasting connections. The science of growth, wired into our name.

About RelnAI
A name with
two meanings.

Reln Holdings LLC is a founder-led, Ohio-based mobile app company focused on building products that gamify everyday life. We are a focused company with an expanding product roadmap.

The name "Reln" was chosen deliberately — it holds a personal truth and a scientific one. Both point to the same north star: connection, growth, and the patterns that make us who we are.
